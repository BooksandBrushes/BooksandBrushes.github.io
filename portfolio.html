AI Comparison Project

For this project, I was assigned to evaluate ten pieces of my writing content. I chose to evaluate five pieces of artificial intelligence (AI) related academic writings and five pieces of non AI related academic writings. I ran the content through two different readbility analyzer applications. One of them was called AntConc and the other was Datayze. I then compared the readbility and quality of my AI-related academic writings v.s my non-AI related academic writings. Using MRAD format, applying plain language techniques and using active voice I ended up getting positive feedback on this project.
